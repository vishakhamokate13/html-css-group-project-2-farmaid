🌱 What are Crop Issues?

Crop issues are problems that affect plant growth and reduce production. These include diseases, pests, lack of nutrients, and water problems.

If farmers identify these issues early, they can protect crops and improve yield.

🌿 How to Manage Crop Problems?

Proper farm management helps reduce crop damage and increases production. Farmers should follow modern and traditional techniques together.

  • ✔ Regular field inspection
  • ✔ Use organic fertilizers
  • ✔ Proper irrigation system
  • ✔ Use pest control methods
